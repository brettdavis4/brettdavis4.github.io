About

My name is Brett Davis and I’m a web developer, an Apple Fanboy, a life-long resident of Indianapolis, an animal lover and the owner of two silly fur ball cats.

For over the past decade, I have worked in various industries around Indianapolis, IN, building high-end web sites and applications. These opportunities gave me the chance to learn new methodologies in development and meet some really great people. Currently, I am working at Fusion Alliance as a Consultant.

When I’m not working, I spend my time surfing the interwebs under the username brettdavis4 (facebook, linkedin, google+, twitter, foursquare, and various message boards). I also have a personal fitness blog: How I Stay In Shape.

When I’m not in front of a computer, I’m working out, running, playing basketball, and practicing the piano.

Projects

TODO
The TODO Project was my final project for O'Reilly's Javascript: JSON and Ajax course. It is a simple TODO list application. It is a plain vanilla JavaScript Application.

Workout Planner
The Workout Planner was my final project for O'Reilly's Advanced JavaScript Essentials course. The Workout Planner allows a user to plan a 6 week workout regimen. It is a plain vanilla JavaScript Application.

Burger Joints
The Buger Joints app was the final project in Udacity's JavaScript Design Patterns course. The app displays places that serve cheeseburgers in Indianapolis, IN on a Google Map. It also shows the Yelp rating and my personal thoughts on the location. It is written in Knockout.js.

Calorie Counter
The Calorie Counter app was the final project in Udacity's Learn Backbone.js course. This Calorie Counter allows a user to track their calories and what they eat. The app also stores a user to store their daily eating history. It is written in BackBone.js.
